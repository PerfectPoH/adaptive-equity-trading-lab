# Commercial Demo Field Questions

Use this checklist only if requesting a trial/demo from FactSet, LSEG/I/B/E/S, Citeline/BioMedTracker, Evaluate, or a similar provider.

## Earnings / PEAD

- Do you provide point-in-time consensus estimates as they existed before the earnings announcement?
- Do records include estimate timestamps, revision timestamps, actual EPS, report time, and BMO/AMC classification?
- Are delisted companies retained in historical coverage?
- Can the sample include at least AEHR, ARRY, CABA, CRMD, IOVA plus a broader biotech/small-cap sample?
- Can we export via API/CSV with stable identifiers?

## Biotech / PDUFA

- Do you provide historical PDUFA dates as known before the decision date?
- Do records include source timestamp, event type, drug, indication, company identifier, and later outcome?
- Are delayed, withdrawn, missed, and failed catalysts retained?
- Are delisted or acquired companies retained?
- Is there an API or bulk export route suitable for backtesting?

## Intraday / Dollar-Bar

- What schemas are available: trades, quotes, MBP, MBO, or order-by-order?
- Are timestamps exchange-normalized and session-aware?
- Are condition codes sufficient to filter odd-lot, auction, and corrected trades?
- What is the estimated cost for a 10-symbol, 3-month micro-probe?
