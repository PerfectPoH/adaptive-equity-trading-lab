# Universe Expansion Gate 001 - 2026-05-24

This gate defines the requirements for expanding the research universe beyond the five-symbol sandbox.

It does not authorize provider queries, market-data downloads, strategy backtests, parameter sweeps, paper trading, live trading, or promotion. Its only purpose is to prevent rare-event strategies from being tested on an undersized universe or on a survivorship-biased ticker list.

The next executable step must be a separate provider-specific universe-source probe with explicit scope, endpoint, call limits, and retention policy.
