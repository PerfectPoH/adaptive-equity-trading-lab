# Research Program Transition Policy Gate - 2026-05-24

No-query governance gate that transitions the lab after the small-cap/free-data directional research campaign.

Policy:

- Small-cap/free-data directional multi-day research is paused.
- Small-cap remains allowed only for microstructure, catalyst, data-quality, and risk diagnostics.
- ETF/large-cap research is opened only as a clean-data risk/regime/portfolio laboratory, not as an "easy alpha" claim.
- No strategy promotion is allowed from active-only or survivorship-biased results.

This gate performs no provider query, no market-data download, no strategy backtest, and no promotion.
