# Polygon Active-Only Exploratory Policy Gate - 2026-05-24

No-query governance gate for active-only exploratory research.

The prior delisted listing-date probe showed that Polygon/Massive does not provide enough delisted listing-date coverage to support a full survivorship-free historical PIT universe with the current free data stack. This policy gate allows only active-only exploratory research under explicit caveats:

- no survivorship-free claim
- no strategy promotion
- no live or paper trading
- no claim of institutional validity
- no broad survivorship-free backtest

It performs no provider query, no market-data download, and no strategy backtest.
